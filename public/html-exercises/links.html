WILDCARDS: A sure way to get on my good side

Watch these with me or no popcorn for you

Let's work our way backwards, shall we? Earning the bronze medal for being my third favorite movie - Flipped, a movie wherein two eighth-graders proved that indeed, opposites really do attract. At first I thought this movie would just be meh since I've read the book; we all know what happens to books-gone-movies. But after giving into the constant pleads of my main nigga to watch it, I realized that it was actually a good movie. The movie had an almost-childlike innocence to it, downplaying the cheesiness of the story itself. Aside from that, the cinematography and the original soundtrack of the movie was very spot on. An added bonus: a good-looking lead actor and the wardrobe of the lead actress that is to die for.
