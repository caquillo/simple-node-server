About

  • local_pizzaEllo is...

    Ello is a beautiful & ad-free social network where you can connect with friends, be inspired, and share what matters most. Ello’s gorgeous fullscreen design supports high-res images, long form text, GIFs, video, sound files — you name it — without the clutter you’ll find on other ad-based social networks.

    Based from the quoted description aforementioned, seems like there isn't really anything different with Ello - except for legit being 100% ad-free. Believe me, I have an account lol. Anyway, their layout is pretty dope in my opinion, being a sucker for minimal design and structure.

  • local_atm'Art not Ads'

    Art not Ads is an Ello-funded initiative that supports artists making art about issues that matter to the Ello community

    All I can say is this is such a great opportunity for budding artists of all type, and actually a nice way of spending money :) With 2016 just around the corner, users should have a heads up soon on the opening for next year's grant schedule.

  • verified_userFriends VS Noise

    With other social networks, you have to sift through countless of posts before you catch a glimpse of people you actually mind, like you honestly pay attention to. With Ello, all you need to do is to decide whether to label them as a FRIEND or NOISE. With other users labeled as friends, you'll instansly see their posts in expanded, list format; while, for those 'unfortunately' labeled as noise, their posts will appear compressed, in a fluid-grid based layout for easy 'once overs'

  • faceEmojis wieee!

    I don't know about you but this is some amazing piece of shit - Ello supports emoji! Users only have to type a ':' then a pop-up menu will appear with emoji choices. Ello recently concluded an emoji contest and the winning emojis are dope; think Spock's signature hand sign *prosper*, doge, a literal representation of hot-shit, and even Kim Jong Un!
